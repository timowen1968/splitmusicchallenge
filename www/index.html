About the game X
Objective
The objective of the game is to guess the title and artist of 10 songs based on a small snippet of each.
Remember that sometimes the song may be a cover, and you need to name the artist performing it here. And, of course, some songs have quiet moments in them, so if you don't hear anything, bummer - you will have to use one of the lifelines mentioned below to help you out.
 
Scoring
You get points for each correct word.
You get bonus points if you get the song and artist.
You get major bonus points if you complete the full challenge.
 
Sharing on Facebook will get you additional points too.
 
Life Lines
If you get stuck, you have a few options.
While a game is in progress, clicking the will replay the snippet for that song. Gonna cost you though. Clicking this button when the game is over will let you listen to more of the song in case something catches your fancy.
 
Otherwise, there are the life lines at the top right of the screen.
Replay won't cost you anything, and replays the full 10 second snippet.
Play a longer snippet can be used a couple of times, but you lose more points each time.
Play a different snippet of the songs might just hit a note that you recognise.
 
Ending the game
The game can end in two ways.
Surrender: The points you've earned will be added to your total, and a new game will start. Nothing bad will happen.
Succeed: Getting all the answers right. Obviously, this is the one to go for. A nice hefty bonus if you get it right.
 
You start off with a rank of Bambi, but don't worry, that will change the more you play and the more points you collect.
 
Purchase
The purchased version has additional features such as:
Select your favourite decade
Select your favourite genre
Select the most popular music
 
Reminder
Clicking the buttons when the game is over will play more of the song for you to listen to.
